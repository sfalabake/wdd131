Nigeria Info & Weather Hub

Site Planning Document - Solomon Oluwadunsin Falabake

Site Name

NaijaInfo & Weather Hub

I chose this name because "Naija" is a popular, friendly term for Nigeria, and it clearly communicates that the site provides both general country information and real-time weather updates.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a digital gateway to Nigeria, providing users with essential national facts (population, currency, history) and real-time weather data for major cities to help travelers and citizens stay informed.

Scenarios

  • "I am traveling to Abuja tomorrow; what is the current temperature and wind speed there?"
  • "I need to know the official population and currency of Nigeria for a school project."

Color Scheme

The colors chosen reflect the Nigerian national identity and provide high contrast for readability.

Primary: #008751 (Green)
Secondary: #ffffff (White)
Accent: #212121 (Dark Gray)

Green is used for headers and navigation; White is for page backgrounds; Dark Gray is for body text to ensure accessibility.

Typography

Headings: Montserrat (Sans-serif) - used for all H1, H2, and H3 elements for a bold, authoritative feel.

Body Text: Open Sans (Sans-serif) - used for all paragraphs and list items for high legibility.
