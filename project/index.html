About Nigeria

The Giant of Africa: Nigeria is a vibrant nation on the Gulf of Guinea, known for its massive economy and rich cultural diversity. With over 250 ethnic groups, it is a melting pot of traditions, music, and innovation.

Geography & Climate: From the tropical rainforests in the South to the arid plains of the North, Nigeria's weather is as diverse as its people. The country primarily experiences a rainy season and a dry season, influenced by the Harmattan trade winds from the Sahara.
