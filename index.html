Lagos, Nigeria

Official Flag of Nigeria

Nigeria, often hailed as the "Giant of Africa," is the continent's most populous nation and a powerhouse of cultural and economic influence. It is home to over 250 ethnic groups, creating a rich tapestry of languages, music, and traditions. Lagos, where I reside, is a bustling megacity and the economic heart of West Africa, famous for its tech hubs, vibrant nightlife, and entrepreneurial energy. Nigeria is also globally recognized for its massive film industry (Nollywood) and its significant contributions to global music and literature.
